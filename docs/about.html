About Me

And the Reason for this Blog

Who am I?

First of all, the most important information. My name is Steven Klotz originating from Germany and being a diligent student is my profession. This fall I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering from the Technical University of Munich. Since then I am enrolled for the Master of Science.

Why am I in Australia?

The TU Munich maintains a multitude of connections to partner universities around the globe. In my case the faculty of Electrical Engineering offers a DoubleDegree cooperation with the University of Queensland. To experience an exciting and unforgettable Master curriculum while progressing academically I decided to participate. In the context of the DoubleDegree framework I will graduate in the fall of 2020 with a master's degree from the University of Queensland as well as TU Munich.

Why does this website exist?

The answer is simple and complicated at the same time. I don't know. Possibly some people are interested in my pictures, stories and thoughts.
